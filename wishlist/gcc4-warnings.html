PuTTY doesn't build cleanly using GCC 4 on Unix because we turn on -Wall and -Werror. Most of the problems are the usual ones to do with signenedness of pointer targets. Debian bug #287960 has some details and patches.

Update: The 2007-01-10 snapshot compiled cleanly with GCC 4.1.2ish.
